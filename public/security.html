How We Protect Your Documents

End-to-End Encryption

All documents are encrypted with AES-256 encryption—the same standard used by banks and governments.

  • TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
  • AES-256 encryption at rest
  • Zero-knowledge architecture

Secure Infrastructure

Hosted on AWS with military-grade physical security and 99.99% uptime SLA.

  • Multi-region redundancy
  • Automated backups every 6 hours
  • 24/7 infrastructure monitoring

Access Controls

Strict role-based access control (RBAC) ensures only authorized personnel can access systems.

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Principle of least privilege
  • Audit logs for all access

Data Minimization

We only keep your documents as long as necessary and delete them automatically.

  • Documents deleted after 30 days
  • Analysis results deleted after 90 days
  • Secure deletion (DOD 5220.22-M standard)

Regular Security Audits

Independent third-party audits and penetration testing every quarter.

  • Annual SOC 2 Type II audits
  • Quarterly penetration tests
  • Continuous vulnerability scanning

Privacy by Design

Your documents are NEVER used to train our AI without explicit consent.

  • No data sharing with third parties
  • Opt-in for AI training data
  • Full transparency in data usage

Security Incident Response

We have a dedicated security team monitoring threats 24/7. In the unlikely event of a breach:

  • You'll be notified within 72 hours
  • We'll provide a detailed incident report
  • We'll work with law enforcement if necessary
  • We'll implement additional safeguards immediately

Our Commitment to Security

All employees undergo security training and sign strict NDAs. Only essential personnel have access to production systems.

We have a documented incident response plan tested quarterly. Our team is trained to respond to security incidents within minutes.
