Making Legal Documents Accessible to Everyone

We believe no one should sign something they don't understand. That's why we built LawGeeks.

Our Story

LawGeeks was born from a simple frustration: legal documents are unnecessarily complicated. In 2023, our founders—a software engineer and a recovering lawyer—watched too many friends and family members sign contracts they didn't fully understand.

The problem wasn't intelligence. It was access. Hiring a lawyer to review every document costs hundreds or thousands of dollars. Reading it yourself means hours of googling legal terms you'll forget tomorrow.

We knew AI could solve this. Within months, we built the first version of LawGeeks: an AI that could read any legal document and explain it in plain English. Today, we've analyzed over 2 million documents for 50,000+ users worldwide.

Our Mission

To democratize legal understanding. Every person deserves to know exactly what they're agreeing to before they sign.

Our Vision

A world where legal language is no longer a barrier to fair agreements and informed decisions.

Our Values

Transparency, accessibility, and accuracy. We build tools that empower people, not replace professionals.

Our Promise

Your documents are yours. We never sell your data, train AI on your content without permission, or share your information.
